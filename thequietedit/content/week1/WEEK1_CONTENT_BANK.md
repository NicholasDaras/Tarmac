## CONTENT SPECIFICATIONS

### Image Requirements for Each Post:
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
- **Color Palette:** Warm beige, cream, camel, soft browns
- **Filter:** Reduced saturation, warm tones, soft contrast
- **Text Overlay:** Minimal or none (let images speak)

### Caption Formula:
1. Hook (1 line, stops scroll)
2. Body (2-4 lines, value/story)
3. Question (drives comments)
4. Hashtags (12-16 tags)

### Engagement Targets (Week 1):
- Follow 50 accounts/day (similar aesthetic)
- Like 100 posts/day

- Reply to ALL comments within 2 hours
