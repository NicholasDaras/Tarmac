## 2. VISUAL AESTHETIC ‚Äî MOOD BOARD SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Primary** | Warm Beige | #F5F1E8 | Backgrounds, borders |
| **Secondary** | Cream White | #FAF9F6 | Clean spaces, text bg |
| **Accent** | Deep Brown | #3D2817 | Text, logos, emphasis |
| **Highlight** | Soft Camel | #C4A77D | Buttons, links, accents |
| **Text** | Charcoal | #2C2C2C | Captions, quotes |

### Typography

**Quotes/Captions:**
- Primary: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif)
- Secondary: Playfair Display (classic editorial)

**UI/Graphics:**
- Primary: Montserrat Light (modern, clean)
- Secondary: Inter (legible, neutral)

### Visual Style Rules

**DO:**
- Warm, soft lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Natural textures (linen, wool, leather, wood)
- Classical architecture (columns, arches, moldings)
- Analog elements (film grain, paper textures)
- Negative space (breathing room in images)
- Muted, desaturated tones

**DON'T:**
- Harsh shadows or flash photography
- Neon colors or high saturation
- Modern/minimalist stark aesthetics
- Busy, cluttered compositions
- Logos or branding (unless luxury heritage)

### Image Types (80/20 Rule)

**80% Curation (Reposts with permission/credit):**
- Classical art details
- Museum interiors
- Vintage fashion photography
- Architecture (European estates, libraries)
- Nature (tuscan villas, english gardens)

**20% Creation (Original):**
- Text overlays on aesthetic backgrounds
- Quote graphics
- "This or That" comparison graphics
- Carousel educational content
